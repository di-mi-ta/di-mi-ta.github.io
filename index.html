Tan M. Dinh

I am an AI Resident at VinAI Research, where I research on computer vision and deep generative models, under the supervision of Dr. Binh-Son Hua, Dr. Rang Nguyen, and Dr. Anh Tran.

Before that, I obtained my Bachelor of Engineering Degree on Computer Science in Honors Program with the highest rank at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT) in 2020.

I am interested in computer vision, GAN-inversion and applications such as real face reconstruction and manipulation, language and vision learning such as text-to-image synthesis.

Publications
HyperInverter: Improving StyleGAN Inversion via Hypernetwork
Tan M. Dinh, Anh Tran, Rang Nguyen, Binh-Son Hua
arxiv preprint, 2021
paper / project page / code (coming soon)

We develop a novel entirely encoder-based two-stage StyleGAN inversion method, which can obtain the superiority of reconstruction quality, editability, and fast inference at the same time by employing the idea of hypernetworks.
TISE: A Toolbox for Text-to-Image Synthesis Evaluation
Tan M. Dinh, Rang Nguyen, Binh-Son Hua
arxiv preprint, 2021
paper / project page / code (coming soon)

We identify several pitfalls in the existing evaluation pipeline of text-to-image synthesis models, and propose a set of metrics combining the improved versions of existing metrics and new ones to form a unified evaluation toolbox, so-called TISE, for benchmarking text-to-images models fairly, robustly, and consistently.
